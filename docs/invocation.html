Invocation


Intended to create a rich soundscape through meditative invocation, Invocation weaves together eclectic world music with the tradition of a spiritual practice. The result is an album that creates contemplative calm and an invitation to feel That Which Is All-Pervading.

Dedicating this album to her spiritual teacher and world-friend, Adi Da Samraj - upon hearing it, Adi Da Samraj requested that Invocation be placed on his "Favorites" list on his personal iPod. Also available as downloadable MP3s from The Dawn Horse Press here, and iTunes.
